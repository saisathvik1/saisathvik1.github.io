I started learning ethical hacking after being inspired by Elliot Alderson from the *Mr. Robot* series. Over the years, I've accumulated a variety of gadgets, and I’d like to briefly cover what each of them does.

## Alfa AW036NHA

- The Alfa AW036NHA was the first hacking gadget I ever owned. I bought it in India for 4,500 rupees, which is roughly 60 USD.
- This WiFi adapter is highly regarded for its Atheros AR9271 chipset, which is known for its reliability in various hacking tasks.
- It supports Monitor Mode and Master Mode on 2.4 GHz networks. The Master Mode feature allows for the creation of multiple virtual interfaces, making it ideal for performing Man-in-the-Middle (MITM) attacks, such as Evil-Twin attacks.
- Like many beginners in the field, I started my journey with WiFi hacking. I learned how to use tools like Aircrack-ng, and perform Evil-Twin attacks and fake firmware attacks using this adapter.
- However, the main limitation of this device is that it only supports attacks on 2.4 GHz WiFi networks.

> I’m planning to upgrade to a new adapter soon, one that supports both 2.4 GHz and 5 GHz frequencies and offers Master Mode capabilities.
